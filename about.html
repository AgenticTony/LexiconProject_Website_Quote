The Face Behind the Brand

My name is Mercy Olaigbe, I am 34 years old and live in Manchester but originally from Nigeria. I am a self-taught cake decorator and cake tutor who has been decorating cakes for almost 5 years. I am a mum of 2 gorgeous boys.

Balancing the joy of motherhood with my love for creating edible art has been one of the most fulfilling parts of my journey. As a tutor, I'm driven by a desire to share my knowledge and empower others to bring their cake dreams to life.

Being a mum has taught me patience, creativity, and resilience‚Äîqualities that flow into my cake artistry and teaching style. I believe in fostering a supportive learning environment where my students not only master techniques but also build confidence in their abilities.

From Passion to Profession

CakesForAll is a luxury cake brand based in Manchester, UK; founded with a passion for creating unforgettable cake experiences in 2020, we are renowned for crafting luxurious, stunning, and uniquely glamorous cake designs. We combine artistry with exceptional flavour to create cakes that are as delicious as they are beautiful.

Our dedication to quality and creativity sets us apart, and we believe every cake tells a story. What began as a passionate hobby has grown into a thriving business that not only delivers exceptional cakes but also inspires bakers globally to elevate their baking and decorating skills.
